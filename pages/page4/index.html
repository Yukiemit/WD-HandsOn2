Aquaman

Home is calling

Get Ticket Watch Trailer

A half-Atlantean, half-human who is reluctant to be king of the undersea nation of Atlantis. He is a member of the Justice League. He possesses superhuman strength, durability, has hydrokinetic powers, can communicate with sea creatures, and can swim at supersonic speeds.
